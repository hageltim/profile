Overview

I have been with C.H. Robinson and the rotational development team since the start of 2017. In my time so far, I have learned a lot about how the rotational team can help other teams grow and progress through times of high demand. As a rotational developer, my goal is to continue to strive to be highly flexible and willing to adapt to new situations. As the late Bruce Lee once said...




“You must be shapeless, formless, like water. When you pour water in a cup, it becomes the cup. When you pour water in a bottle, it becomes the bottle. When you pour water in a teapot, it becomes the teapot. Water can drip and it can crash. Become like water my friend.”

About Me

I grew up in West Fargo, ND and it wasn't until spring 2016 when I moved to the Twin Cities area. I have two cats named Apollo and Leo. I love watching movies and playing both video games and tabletop games. I enjoy to go camping and hiking, or anything that allows me to spend more time enjoying nature. I like going to Sci-fi/Fantasy and Gaming conventions, it's generally a great time and you get to meet a lot of new people. I am a cereal connoisseur, I will admit to going down the cereal aisle just to see if there are any interesting new cereals.

Video Games

The Nintendo Entertainment System was my first game console that I shared with my older brother and sister growing up. Ever since then, I've been hooked and I continue to play both new and old games today. Early on, I began collecting older video games, even those from long before my time. My oldest system is a Magnavox Odyssey 200 from 1975. I love game history and I enjoy finding time to experience the roots of video gaming. During my time in Fargo, I was the E-Gaming Coordinator for 5 years for CoreCon.

Music

One big item on my life's bucket list is to learn the Piano and write at least one song. I've done some self teaching when I was younger, but I never had the time or money to take lessons. I'm hoping to change that soon. The thing I love about the piano is how versatile it can become when combined with the capabilities of computers. With today's technology, a single person could create their own orchestral symphony and share it with the world. I also love to hunt for new music. I try to explore as many genres as I can, but a few I often return to are Industrial/EBM, Metal, Chiptune, and Punk music.
